->Run the make file 
->./game
->In the game the semicircle to the left top corner is the speedometer,the angle increases as the speed increases.
-> The Black stars are the target.
->There are five lifes.
->All the targets should be hit in 5 lifes.
->The score and the number of lifes are shown in the terminal.
-> The horizontal pieces below the stars are immovable obstacles.The rebounds if it hits them.
->The verical shapes are movable obstacles.
->the game will be over if all the five lives are used or if are targets are hit.
